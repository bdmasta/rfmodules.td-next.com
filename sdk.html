SDK 5.0.1 main features

TD12xx have a state-of-the-art ARM Cortex M3, which is enough to embbeded the majority of M2M and IoT applications.

The SDK includes a TD12xx EVB and adds an Energy Micro STK3300 starter kit, used as a JTAG adapter.

A complete toolchain and IDE (Integrated Development Environment) based on Eclipse are provided free of charge.

Software ressources and documentations required to program your own application into the TD module are provided in order to enable access to all embedded Cortex ARM Cortex M3 Microcontroller, GPIOs, I2C bus, timers with full access to the TD next libraries.
